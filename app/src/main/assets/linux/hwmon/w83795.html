Kernel driver w83795¶

Supported chips:

  • Winbond/Nuvoton W83795G

    Prefix: 'w83795g'

    Addresses scanned: I2C 0x2c - 0x2f

    Datasheet: Available for download on nuvoton.com

  • Winbond/Nuvoton W83795ADG

    Prefix: 'w83795adg'

    Addresses scanned: I2C 0x2c - 0x2f

    Datasheet: Available for download on nuvoton.com

Authors:

  • Wei Song (Nuvoton)

  • Jean Delvare <jdelvare@suse.de>
